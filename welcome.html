Online Railway Reservation System is developed for to automate the railways reservation system. It includes modules required to successfully operate railways reversion process smoothly. It has train master to add modified train information, train schedule to enter train journey details include all the station name, arrival time and departure time. It includes automatic fair calculation as per the distance between two stations. Reservation module consists of automatic seat number and coaches no allocation system. Daily schedule for updating of not conform seat and coach no. All master like train master, Train schedule, reservation fees, cancellation fees, charges can be modified individually from front end and changes reflect in all modules immediately. Therefore proposed “Online Railway Reservation System” has been designed to automate the process of railway for ticket reservation and back office activities. System can make the daily activities efficient and provide the fast response.
